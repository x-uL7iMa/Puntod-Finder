About Puntod Finder

Puntod Finder is a digital tool created to help families, friends, and visitors easily locate the final resting place of their loved ones at San Pedro Bautista Memorial Park II.

This website provides a searchable database tomb locations and an interactive map (coming soon).

About San Pedro Bautista Memorial Park II

San Pedro Bautista Memorial Park II is a peaceful and beautifully maintained cemetery located in Candelaria, Quezon. The park serves as the final resting place for many cherished individuals and families.

Contact / Developer Info

This project is developed by a group of students, as part of a learning journey to help the local community through digital tools.
